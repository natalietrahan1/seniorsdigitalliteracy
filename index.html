About our Program

Learn essential digital skills with confidence. Our patient, supportive approach makes technology easy for seniors.

What We Offer:

  • Device & App Basics: Turn devices on/off, navigate menus, use email, browsers, and messaging apps.
  • Connectivity: Securely connect to Wi-Fi.
  • Simple Interface: Understand icons, settings, and terms easily.
  • Troubleshooting: Basic tips for common tech issues.
  • Internet Safety
    • Scam Prevention: Identify phishing, fake calls, and fraud.
    • Social Media: Use platforms safely, manage privacy.
    • Recognizing Catfishing: Recognize and avoid deceptive online relationships.
    • Password Security: Create strong, secure passwords.
    • Safe Transactions: Shop and bank online confidently.
    • Reporting: Know how to report suspicious activity.

Our Approach:

  • Patient and Personalized: Step-by-step guidance.
  • Hands-On: Learn by doing.
  • Simplified Language: No technical jargon.
  • Small Group Settings: Focused attention & Q&A.

Benefits:

  • Gain digital independence and confidence.
  • Stay connected with loved ones.
  • Access online resources for hobbies, health, and information.
  • Protect yourself from online threats.
  • Enjoy the digital age with peace of mind.

Services Available

Learn the essentials of computers and smartphones, and gain confidence in staying safe online.

Computer Basics

Help you feel comfortable using your computer.

We'll cover how to turn it on and off, use the mouse and keyboard, open and close programs, and find your way around the screen, making everyday tasks simple.

Learn more

Smartphone Basics

Help you feel comfortable using your phone.

We'll cover how to make calls, send messages, use popular apps, connect to Wi-Fi, and manage your contacts, making it easy to stay connected and explore.

Learn more

Online Safety

Help you navigate the internet with confidence.

We'll teach you how to spot and avoid common scams, protect your personal information from falling into the wrong hands, and use social media safely, so you can enjoy the online world without worry.
