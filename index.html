About the Program

This volunteer-led program provides clear and supportive instruction for seniors who want to feel more confident using technology. Our focus is on practical skills, online safety, and protection from scams and fraud.

What We Teach

Computer Basics

Using a mouse and keyboard, understanding screens, email, and basic internet use.

Smartphone Help

Making calls, sending texts, installing apps, and adjusting settings.

Online Safety

Recognizing scams, protecting personal information, and staying safe online.

Our Volunteers

Volunteers act as friendly guides and helpers. No technical certification is required. Training and teaching materials are provided.
